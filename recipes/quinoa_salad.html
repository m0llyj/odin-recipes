Quinoa Salad

A bowl of quinoa salad.

This recipe is a great choice for a light summer dinner. With rainbow quinoa, beans, and vegetables, you will enjoy a colorful dish with all the nutrients you need.

Quinoa salad comes together quickly and easily, making it an easy process to cook.

Ingredients

  • 1 c uncooked quinoa
  • 3 c broth
  • 2 cans of chickpeas
  • 1 can of corn
  • 4 minced cloves of garlic
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 cans black beans
  1. Cook quinoa according to package directions, using broth instead of water.
  2. Once cooked, stir in the minced garlic and lime juice.
  3. Drain canned ingredients, then add to quinoa and mix.
